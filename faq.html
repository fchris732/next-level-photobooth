Frequently Asked Questions

How much space and power do you need?

Most setups need an 8’×8’ area and one 120V outlet within 15 feet. 360 needs more room.

Do you provide an attendant?

Yes. A trained attendant sets up, assists guests, and keeps the line moving.

Can we customize overlays and backdrops?

Yes. We’ll match your theme and provide proofs before the event.

Do you travel?

We serve NJ, NYC, and Eastern PA. Travel fees may apply by distance.

What’s the booking process?

Check availability, approve quote, sign agreement, and pay a retainer to lock in your date.
