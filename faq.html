Frequently Asked Questions

How much space and power do you need?

We typically require an 8′ × 8′ area and one standard 120 V outlet within 15 feet. Our 360 XL needs a bit more room.

Do you provide an attendant?

Yes, a trained attendant will set up the booth, assist guests and keep things running smoothly throughout your event.

Can we customize overlays and backdrops?

Absolutely! We work with you to match your event theme. We’ll send proofs of your overlays and backdrops before the big day.

Do you travel?

We serve New Jersey, New York City and Eastern Pennsylvania. Travel fees may apply for events outside this area.

What’s the booking process?

First check availability and get a quote. Then we’ll send an agreement to sign and ask for a retainer to lock in your date.
